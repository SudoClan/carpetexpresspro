Our Services

Carpet Cleaning

In our carpet shop we have a vast selection of domestic carpet samples to suit every taste. We have a superb selection of quality carpets, in all the popular styles including Twists, Berbers, Cords, Plains, Patterns and natural textures.

  • Quality underlays guaranteed for the lifetime of your carpet.
  • Discounts off full roll orders.
  • FREE measures and estimates.
  • Full professional fitting service.

The staff in our shop have years of experience in the flooring trade and can offer you the best advice if you aren’t certain on what type of carpet will best suit your home. We understand that buying carpets for your home isn’t something that should be hurried so you’re welcome to ask as many questions as you like and we will only give you our honest, professional advice.

Vinyl Flooring

One of the most affordable and resilient flooring solutions. Easy installation, versatility, low maintenance and high durability make vinyl a popular flooring choice. We can provide vinyls in 2, 3 and 4 metre widths with a variety of colours and patterns to match the decorating style of any room.

We have a large choice of vinyl and can easily show you various samples in your own home. We supply a hassle free service with full guarantees and fitting to British Standards. Preparation is very important when fitting vinyl flooring; the result of a vinyl is reflective of the concrete floor or floor boards below. We can fully prepare an area at a very inexpensive price.

Laminate Flooring

Warm, environmentally-friendly, natural looking flooring, which can be laid with ease and the minimum of mess.

We offer:

  • A superb range of authentic styles to suit your budget.
  • Laminate flooring with perfectly closed joints using the licensed uniclic glueless jointing system.
  • Comfortable flooring which can be used immediately after installation.

Smooth surface floor coverings can be the ideal choice for selected areas in the home such as kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, dining rooms, conservatories, etc.  They can offer a powerful combination of visual impact and practicality. The choice of designs, textures, wear rating and price levels of this group of floor coverings can be at first glance mind-boggling!  The nature and condition of your sub-floors can also have a particularly strong bearing on your choice of smooth floor covering. However, our free Home Visits, on-site surveys and advice ensures that choosing the perfect smooth surface floor covering for your home is a relaxing and pleasurable experience.
